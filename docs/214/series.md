## What they is...
The concept of a series is tightly coupled to sequences. They are simply the
summation of all of the terms of sequence.

$$
\begin{align*}
a_n = \{n\} && \text{a sequence} \\
\sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n && \text{a series}
\end{align*}
$$
